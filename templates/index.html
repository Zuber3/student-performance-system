Smart Student Performance
Analysis System

A smart DBMS + Artificial Intelligence based system specially designed for Guardian Faculty Members (GFM), Subject Teachers, and Students to efficiently monitor and improve academic performance. The system stores student records, attendance, marks, and academic progress using SQLite Database, while an intelligent prediction engine analyzes performance to identify students who may need academic support. It helps GFMs and teachers make better decisions, track progress easily, and guide students toward improved academic success

AI Grade Prediction
